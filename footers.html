Footers

A footer is a great way to add additional content to your web apps. Tuxedo's footer is simple, straightforward, and easily customized. Give it a whirl.

Basic Footer

To use the footer place it at the very bottom of your body tag below your other content.

Item 1 Item 2 Item 3 Item 4
<footer class="f-row bg-dark text-light responsive"> <small class="f-col-3 flex-center">Item 1</small> <small class="f-col-3 flex-center">Item 2</small> <small class="f-col-3 flex-center">Item 3</small> <small class="f-col-3 flex-center"> Item 4</small> </footer>

Double Decker Footer

This option is great if there are a few more items you want to add to your footer.
