Sidebar

Sidebars are an alternative to the traditional navbar. You can see our sidebar at work right here on this webpage. Right out of the box - the sidebar disappears on smaller screen sizes. This is because content tends to look squished on tablet and mobile devices when confined in a sidebar. We suggest pairing the sidebar with a mobile navbar for smaller screensizes. The bottom example will show you how.

Sidebars come with their own container where you can put the rest of your content. This container keeps the sidebar from running into your content on various screen sizes.
